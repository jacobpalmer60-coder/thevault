How to publish your Google Sheet
  1. Open your Google Sheet with columns: sleeper_id, name, team, pos, age, number, value, ppg, ppg_source, last_updated
  2. File → Share → Publish to web
  3. Choose the Values tab (or your rankings tab)
  4. Format: Comma-separated values (.csv)
  5. Click Publish and copy the link
  6. Paste the link above and hit Load (then Save)
Tips
  • • URL should contain output=csv or end with pub?output=csv
  • • Cache-busting is automatic (?t= timestamp)
  • • Data stays in your browser only. Sheet must be public.
  • • Export button downloads the currently filtered view.
