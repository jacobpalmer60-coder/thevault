How to use

  1. Load your Sleeper league via username → select league.
  2. Upload a CSV with player_id,value,ppg or name,value,ppg.
  3. Pick Team A & Team B from dropdowns.
  4. Add players to trade from rosters. Summary updates live with PPG & optimal lineup impact.
  5. Fairness verdict & warnings appear at top of center panel.
