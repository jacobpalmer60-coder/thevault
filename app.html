Tier Explanations

  • Elite: Top-end starters at QB/RB/WR, deep benches, multiple 1sts. Championship favorite.
  • Strong: Clear playoff team with star power. May need depth or one positional upgrade.
  • Average: Fringe playoff squad. Reliant on breakouts or health to contend.
  • Weak: Aging core or shallow starters. Best path is retool around youth/picks.
  • Rebuild: Bottom 2 in value. Liquidate veterans for future picks and young upside.
