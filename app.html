Dynasty League Overview

Methodology Show methodology ▼

Prod % = percentile of optimal starting lineup PPG (based on Avg_PPR_0.5_TEP). This is how good your best possible starters are right now.

Window % = percentile of 0.5*valueZ + 0.3*picksZ + 0.2*(-ageZ). Value = sum of player dynasty values from your Age CSV. Picks = sum of future pick values, pulled year-specific from your sheet so 2027 Late 1st ≠ 2028 Late 1st. Age = average roster age.

Playoff-aware: 6 playoff teams → contender Prod floor = 40th percentile. Window thresholds stay 30 / 70. Elite remains Prod ≥70 & Window ≥70.

Archetypes

Elite Contender – Prod ≥70, Window ≥70, age ≤26.5. Strong now and built to last.
Win-Now Fringe – Prod ≥40, Window 30-70, age ≥26. Playoff-caliber now.
Aging Contender – Prod ≥40, Window <30, age ≥27.5, picksPct ≤30. Win now, window closing.
Young Riser – Prod 30-70, Window ≥70, age ≤25.5. Elite future.
Pick-Rich Rebuilder – Prod <40, Window ≥70, picksPct ≥70.
Volatile – Prod 30-70, Window <30.
Stripped Rebuilder – Prod <40, Window <30.
Stuck in the Middle – Prod <40, Window 30-70.
Balanced Core – No extremes.
