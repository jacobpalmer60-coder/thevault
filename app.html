Methodology

Production - How good are you right now?

We take your roster and pick the best starters based on roster settings. Add up their projected points per game using Avg_PPR_0.5_TEP. That is your Starters PPG. Prod % is your percentile rank in the league. High = win now.

Window - How long can you stay good?

We look at three things: total roster talent value, draft picks for 2027-2029, and how young your core is. We z-score each, combine as 0.5*valueZ + 0.3*picksZ + 0.2*(-ageZ), then rank vs league. High = bright future.

Archetypes

  • Elite Contender – Top production now and strong future.
  • Aging Contender – Wins now but core is old.
  • Win-Now Fringe – Good starters, weak future assets.
  • Young Riser / Ascending – Rising production with youth and picks.
  • Pick-Rich Rebuilder – Lots of future draft capital, low current production.
  • Volatile / High-Variance – Middling with boom/bust profile.
  • Stripped Rebuilder – Low production and few picks.
  • Stuck in the Middle / Purgatory – Average everywhere, no clear direction.
  • Balanced Core – Solid across the board, no extremes.
