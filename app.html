Tier System

Tiers are valuation bands derived from the sheet's Value column. Elite, Core, Depth, and Dart.

Elite: Top 24 overall. League-winning difference makers.
Core: Reliable top 75 starters with multi-year windows.
Depth: Startable in pinch, strong bye fill.
Dart: Upside stashes, rookie picks, injured discounts.
PPG: Optimal starters sum. Zero-PPG players are filtered unless needed to fill.
Picks: Years sorted ascending, 2026 excluded.
