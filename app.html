Tier Explanations

  • Tier 1: Franchise cornerstones. Top-12 dynasty assets with elite production and multi-year window.
  • Tier 2: Proven weekly starters. High floor + ceiling, safe building blocks.
  • Tier 3: Solid starters with minor questions. Good trade targets.
  • Tier 4: Depth with upside. Flex-viable or developmental.
  • Tier 5: Bench stashes. Need path to relevance.
  • Tier 6: Dart throws. Long shots or age cliffs.

How It Works

  • Values & PPG are pulled exactly from your Google Sheet CSV. Matching by sleeper_id then normalized name.
  • Team VALUE = sum of player values + adjusted rookie pick value.
  • Team PPG = optimal Superflex lineup: QB×2, RB×2, WR×3, TE×1, FLEX×2, SUPERFLEX×1. Hover PPG for slot breakdown.
  • PK = pick capital rank. Hover for pick list. Completed rookie drafts auto-exclude that season's picks from value.
  • Expanded panel shows full roster by position, no truncation. Values shown with commas to avoid k-format confusion.
