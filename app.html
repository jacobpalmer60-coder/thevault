Methodology
Production – Percentile of optimal starter PPG for the current scoring (PPR + 0.5 TEP).
Window – Long-term outlook percentile derived from roster value, pick capital, and age: windowRaw = 0.5·valueZ + 0.3·picksZ + 0.2·(-ageZ).
Archetypes – Assigned from Prod % and Window % plus age and pick capital:
  • Elite Contender: Prod ≥70 & Window ≥70 & Age ≤26.5
  • Aging Contender: Prod ≥70 & Window ≤30 & Age ≥27.5 & Picks ≤30
  • Win-Now Fringe: Prod ≥70 & Window 30-69 & Age ≥26.5
  • Young Riser: Prod 40-69 & Window ≥70 & Age ≤25.5
  • Pick-Rich Rebuilder: Prod ≤40 & Window ≥70 & Picks ≥70
  • Volatile: Prod 40-69 & Window ≤30
  • Stripped Rebuilder: Prod ≤40 & Window ≤30
  • Stuck in Middle: Prod ≤40 & Window 30-69
  • Balanced Core: otherwise
Pick years start at current season, or next season if the rookie draft is complete. Rounds 1-4 only.
