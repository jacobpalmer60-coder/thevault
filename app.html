How Prod % and Window % are calculated

Prod % = percentile of optimal starting lineup PPG (based on Avg_PPR_0.5_TEP for all players in this league).

Window % = percentile of windowRaw = 0.5*valueZ + 0.3*picksZ + 0.2*(-ageZ), where value = sum of player values from Age CSV, picks = pick score from pick value map, age = average roster age. Z-scores are standardized across the league.

Picks are valued for 2027-2029 rounds 1-4 using the Early/Mid/Late tier of the original pick owner (tier derived from first-pass PPG: top 3 = late, middle 4 = mid, bottom 3 = early).

What this means in plain English

Production %

How good your best possible starting lineup is right now. We take each team's optimal starters for your league settings and rank them by projected PPR points per game. 90% means you score more than 90% of the league today – you're a top team this season.

Window %

How strong your long-term future looks. It's a blend of three things: total roster value, future draft picks you own, and how young your roster is. 90% means your dynasty outlook is better than 90% of the league – you're set up to win for years.

Archetypes explained

Teams are labeled by the first archetype they match in priority order:

  • Elite Contender: Strong now and built to last. Top-tier production with a young core and good value.
  • Aging Contender: Wins now but the window is closing. Older roster with few future picks.
  • Win-Now Fringe: Good scoring today but older. Can compete now but not an elite long-term setup.
  • Young Riser / Ascending: Middle-of-the-pack production now with elite future outlook and youth.
  • Pick-Rich Rebuilder: Not scoring much yet, but loaded with future draft capital for a rebuild.
  • Volatile / High-Variance: Decent points now but shaky long-term foundation. Boom or bust.
  • Stripped Rebuilder: Low production and poor future outlook. Full tear-down needed.
  • Stuck in the Middle / Purgatory: Not good now and not great later. Tough spot to improve from.
  • Balanced Core: Solid across the board with no extreme strengths or weaknesses.
