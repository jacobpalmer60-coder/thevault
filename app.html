Tier Explanations

CHAMPIONSHIP WINDOW

Top 25% PPG starters + top 40% total value + prime age core (avg starter age 25-29). Win-now roster with depth.

CONTENDER

Above-median PPG and value. Can compete now but may lack elite upside or depth at a key spot.

COMPETITIVE

Middle of pack. One spike week away, but roster holes or age cliffs limit ceiling.

REBUILDING

High pick capital + Young Core. Value is future-focused. PPG starters below median by design.

TANKING

Low PPG, low value, few premium picks. Needs consolidation and asset acquisition.

Badges

Young Core (3+ ≤25y high-value players), Elite Value (top 25% total), High Scoring (top 25% PPG), QB Strong (≥19 PPG QB), Pick Rich (top 33% pick capital).
