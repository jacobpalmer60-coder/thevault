Tier Explanations

  • Tier 1 - Elite: Top ~10% of league by total roster value. Championship favorite with elite starters and depth across positions.
  • Tier 2 - Contender: Strong core values, playoff lock. May need one impact piece to push to Tier 1.
  • Tier 3 - Competitive: Middle of pack. Can compete weekly but lacks high-end difference makers or has age concerns.
  • Tier 4 - Fringe: Rebuild incoming. Value concentrated in few assets or aging roster with limited picks.
  • Tier 5 - Rebuild: Bottom tier. Best path is to accumulate picks and young upside. Avoid win-now moves.
Values sourced from Google Sheet. PPG and Age are roster averages. PK = adjusted pick value. VALUE = players + picks. All numbers update live.
