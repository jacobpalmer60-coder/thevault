How We Calculate

Opt PPG: We sort your roster by Avg_PPR_0.5_TEP, then fill your league’s starting slots in restrictiveness order: QB → RB → WR → TE → FLEX → SUPER_FLEX → WRRB_FLEX → REC_FLEX. Sum of those starters = Opt PPG. Hover the Opt PPG pill for the exact slot-by-slot list (player • PPG • Value).
Productivity: team Opt PPG ÷ highest Opt PPG in league → 0-100%. Shows how close your best lineup is to the league’s best lineup this season.
Longevity: Value-weighted average age. longevity = clamp(100 − ((avg_age − 23) × 8), 0, 100). 23 = 100%; each year over 23 costs ~8 points. Younger cores score higher.

Archetypes (percentiles)

  • Elite Contender: valP ≥75, ppgP ≥75, age 24-27.5
  • Aging Contender: ppgP ≥70, age ≥27.5
  • Win-Now Fringe: 60 ≤ ppgP <75, age ≥26
  • Young Riser / Ascending: age <25.5, valP ≥45, 40 ≤ ppgP <65
  • Pick-Rich Rebuilder: ppgP <40, age <26, valP ≥35
  • Balanced Core: positional balance ≥70th percentile
  • Volatile / High-Variance: valP ≥70 and ppgP ≤45, or one position ≥50% of value
  • Stripped Rebuilder: valP ≤25 and ppgP ≤25
  • Stuck in the Middle / Purgatory: everything else

valP = Total Value percentile, ppgP = Opt PPG percentile, balance = 1 − std dev of QB/RB/WR/TE value shares.
