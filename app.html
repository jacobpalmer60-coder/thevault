Methodology & Data Sources ▾

Production

Optimal projected PPG based on best-ball lineup optimization using scoring settings (PPR +0.5 TEP). Starters are assigned greedily to the most restrictive slots first (QB,RB,WR,TE → FLEX → SUPER_FLEX → BN).

Window Length

Composite z-score of roster value (50%), draft pick capital (30%), and youth (20%). Pick capital discounts future years 15% per season. Younger age improves window.

Archetypes (9 buckets)

  • Elite Contender: Top-tier production and window, young core. Win now and later.
  • Young Riser / Ascending: Mid production, top window, very young. Trajectory up.
  • Pick-Rich Rebuilder: Low production now, elite window via picks. Asset collection phase.
  • Win-Now Fringe: High production, middling window, older roster. Last push window.
  • Balanced Core: Middle of the pack in both production and window. Decision point.
  • Stuck in the Middle / Purgatory: Low production, middling window. Danger zone.
  • Aging Contender: High production, poor window, old and pick-poor. Fade risk.
  • Volatile / High-Variance: Mid production, poor window. Outcome dependent on hits.
  • Stripped Rebuilder: Bottom in production and window. Full teardown.

Data Sources

Sleeper API v1 (league, users, rosters, players/nfl, traded_picks). Age/Value CSV and PPG CSV from Google Sheets pub.
