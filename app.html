Tier Explanations

Platinum: Top 10% positional strength in league. Elite starters plus premium depth. Championship-level room.
Gold: Top 30%. Strong starters with quality depth. Clear advantage vs average.
Silver: 30-60%. Solid and playable. Not a liability, not a differentiator.
Bronze: 60-85%. Below average. Needs upgrade or relies on breakout.
Iron: Bottom 15%. Roster hole. Immediate priority in trades/draft.
PPG: Sum of projected points per game for the optimal starting lineup based on your league's roster settings (e.g., 1 QB, 2 RB, 3 WR, 1 TE, 2 FLEX). FLEX slots are filled by the best remaining RB/WR/TE after base starters are filled.
